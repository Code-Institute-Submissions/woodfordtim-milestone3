Who We Are

We are triathletes, runners, cyclists and swimmers of all ages and abilities. We swim, bike and run for fun, to stay fit and to socialise with like-minded people

What We Do

Our sole purpose at Tri-Events is to build our triathlon community by sharing events so that others, like you and me, can find the perfect event in which to compete, stay fit and most importantly have fun!

How We Do It

This is a community led website where you can search for the right event for you based on the type of event are looking for, distance, ability and, of course, location. If you are an event organiser or want to share a great event, you can do that, too.
